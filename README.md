# OrchAIstra

# Enterprise AI Governance & Business Analytics Platform

<img width="557" height="304" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/07c35c49-bded-44ce-afd3-495e873d2bee" />



OrchAIstra is an enterprise AI governance and business analytics platform that helps organizations monitor AI adoption, evaluate governance maturity, assess operational risk, and support executive decision-making through interactive dashboards and automated reporting.

This project demonstrates business analytics, data visualization, KPI reporting, and dashboard development using modern analytics tools.

---

## Business Problem

As organizations increasingly adopt AI, they face challenges in monitoring governance, compliance, operational risks, and business performance across multiple departments.

OrchAIstra provides a centralized analytics platform that enables decision-makers to:

- Monitor AI initiatives
- Measure governance maturity
- Evaluate operational risk
- Track business KPIs
- Analyze ESG metrics
- Generate executive reports
